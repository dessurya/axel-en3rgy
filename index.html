About Us

Axel Energy Limited was just founded in Hong Kong in August 2017. We aim to support of biomass energy supply to the global market.

Our commercial office is located in Hong Kong we have a commercial representative in Japan and supply chain management is granted in concert with our Indonesian associate company based in Jakarta.

We arrange reliable and cost-effective supply of palm oil derived products and delivery to the final user in order to capture value along supply chain.

Our supply company based in Indonesia, aim to be a sustainable biomass supplier and to leads the rest of other supply company with its quality, stability, reliable delivery of biomass energy, mainly Palm Kernel Shell, in Indonesia.

In our early stages, we will focus on supplying the biomass energy product from Indonesia to targeting Japan.Then later we have a vision to expand supply clients to other country.

What We Supply

Our supply network, mainly with Government-owned plantation (PTPN), is high-reliability with its long-term contract.

What We Do

Providing consistently management , from purchasing to shipping management.

Purchase / Stock

We own the Stock Pile at Buton, Sumatera and stock our procured product there.

Inspection for Acceptance and Removal of Foreign Substance will be done at the Stock Pile location.

Free space of our stock pile will be available for lease. (to be charged)

Loading

Our Stock Pile is located very close to the loading point of shipment and loading can be executed by our conveyor. Loading system can also be rented.

Shipment Port Management

We provide the shipping arrangement service, to book the vessel and preparation of the document for the shipment and as well as arranging for the following various procedures with port side, securing a berth, obtaining time for navigation control, piloting, tug-boat.
